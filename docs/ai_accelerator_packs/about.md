# About OCI AI Accelerator Packs

AI Accelerator packs are a full deployment OCI resource manager templates that deploy the necessary OCI services and Gen AI applications with optimizations to make Gen AI apps operational. AI Accelerator packs leverage OCI AI Blueprints as a platform to launch and manage the lifecycle of Gen AI applications. For more information please visit [product page.](custom_blueprints/README.md)
